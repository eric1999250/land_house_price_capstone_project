LPES - Land Price Estimation System Knowledge Base

SYSTEM OVERVIEW:
LPES is a Machine Learning-Based Framework for Land Price Estimation in Rwanda.
It covers Huye District, specifically Huye and Mbazi Sectors.
Developed at Rwanda Polytechnic, Huye College, ICT Department.

USERS AND ROLES:
1. Buyer/Seller - Can search land parcels, get price estimates, list land for sale, chat with sellers, submit suggestions
2. District Land Officer - Manages sector notaries, views mutations, generates reports, forwards reports to admin
3. Sector Notary (Government) - Certifies land mutation deeds at sector level, sets appointments, stamps documents, sends to district
4. Private Notary - Independent legal professional who certifies land mutations
5. Admin/System Admin - Manages all users, views stats, manages mutations, reads suggestions and reports

HOW TO SEARCH LAND (UPI):
- UPI stands for Unique Parcel Identifier
- Format: Province/District/Sector/Cell/Parcel (e.g. xx/xx/xx/xx/xxxx)
- No spaces, use forward slashes only
- Go to homepage, enter UPI in search box, click Search

HOW TO ESTIMATE LAND PRICE:
- First search for a UPI
- After land data appears, click "Estimate Land Price" button
- System uses Machine Learning model to estimate Minimum, Average, and Maximum price
- Prices shown in RWF (Rwandan Francs) with price per square meter

TAX CALCULATION:
- Capital Gains Tax rate: 2.5%
- Tax applies ONLY on the amount above 5,000,000 RWF
- Example: Land at 8,000,000 RWF → Taxable amount = 3,000,000 → Tax = 75,000 RWF
- Land below 5,000,000 RWF = No tax

LAND TRANSFER PROCESS (12 Steps):
Step 1: Seller lists land on marketplace
Step 2: Buyer finds listing and chats with seller
Step 3: Seller and buyer agree on price in chat
Step 4: Seller confirms agreement
Step 5: Seller fills Form 11.a (seller information)
Step 6: Buyer fills Form 11.b (buyer information)
Step 7: System sends documents to notary
Step 8: Notary reviews documents
Step 9: Notary sets appointment date for signing
Step 10: Notary uploads certified documents
Step 11: Notary stamps and signs the deed
Step 12: Notary sends completed mutation to district

REGISTRATION:
- Buyers/Sellers can self-register and are auto-approved
- Notaries and Officers must wait for admin/district approval
- Email must be Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook, Hotmail, iCloud, ProtonMail or .rw domain
- Phone must be Rwandan: MTN (078/079) or TIGO (072/073)
- Password must have 8+ chars, uppercase, lowercase, number, special character
- National ID must be 16 digits starting with 1

LOCATION:
- System covers Rwanda, Southern Province, Huye District
- Sectors: Huye and Mbazi
- Data sourced from Rwanda Land Management Authority

TECHNICAL:
- Built with Next.js (frontend) and Flask/Python (backend)
- Machine Learning model predicts land prices
- PostgreSQL database
- Supports English and Kinyarwanda languages
